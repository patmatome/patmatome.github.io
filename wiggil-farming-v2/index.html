Welcome to Wiggil Farming

Located in the fertile lands of Limpopo between Vivo and Aldays, we take pride in growing high-quality products using modern and sustainable farming practices. The Wiggill family has come a long way with farming, Cliffed Wiggill and father Clive farm with Sorghum, Corn, Grapes, Farm Game, Potatoes and butternuts which they supply to two large chain stores. Keeping it in the family, Cliffed’s wife Theressa handles the farm’s finances.
