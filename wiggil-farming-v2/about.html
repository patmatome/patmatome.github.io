About Us

Wiggill Farming – Kudu Ranch

Wiggill Farming, also known as Kudu Ranch, is a family-run agricultural and game farm located between Vivo and Alldays in the heart of the Limpopo bushveld. Since its establishment in 1992, the Wiggill family has built the farm into a balanced operation that blends modern crop production with responsible wildlife and land management.

What We Do

1. Crop Farming
We manage and cultivate around 300 hectares of irrigated farmland, specialising in:Potatoes Butternuts Sorghum (used strategically in rotational cycles to protect soil health)Our approach blends precision farming, modern machinery, and sustainable land practices. The farm uses advanced tractor technology and telemetry systems to optimise efficiency, reduce downtime, and ensure consistent quality.
2. Game Ranching – “Kudu Ranch”
Beyond crops, the farm operates a game section known as Kudu Ranch home to a variety of wildlife.This side of the operation focuses on:
  • Ethical wildlife management
  • Maintaining natural bushveld ecosystems
  • Supporting biodiversity on the property
  • Creating a balance between agriculture and conservation

Our Story

What started as a traditional family farm has grown into a diversified, technology-driven operation. Under the leadership of the Wiggill family’s second generation, the farm has expanded its agricultural capacity while developing a thriving game and wildlife section. The farm’s headquarters sit on the original family property near Vivo, where the commitment to growth, innovation, and integrity has stayed constant for more than three decades.

Our Vision

To build a future-proof farm that produces high-quality crops, supports healthy wildlife ecosystems, and maintains its legacy as a trusted family operation in Limpopo.
